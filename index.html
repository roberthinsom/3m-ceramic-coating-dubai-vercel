What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to a vehicle’s exterior, which bonds with the paintwork to create a durable, hydrophobic layer of protection. This technology has revolutionized the auto detailing industry due to its ability to provide long-lasting protection and enhance the appearance of the car. How Does Ceramic Coating Work?

  1. Preparation: The process begins with a thorough wash and decontamination of the vehicle’s surface to remove dirt, tar, and other impurities. This step is essential to ensure that the coating adheres properly to the paint.
  2. Polishing: After the surface is cleaned, a polishing procedure is carried out to correct any imperfections like swirl marks or light scratches. This step ensures that the surface is smooth and ready for the ceramic coating.
  3. Application: The ceramic coating, such as 3M Ceramic Coating, is then carefully applied by hand, with a focus on achieving an even and flawless coverage over the entire vehicle. This process ensures maximum protection and optimal bonding with the car’s paint.
  4. Curing: After the application, the coating is left to cure for several hours, allowing it to bond effectively with the paint surface. During this time, the coating hardens, creating a protective shield that’s both durable and long-lasting.
